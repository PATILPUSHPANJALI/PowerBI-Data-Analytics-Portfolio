# 📈 SN Corp Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, profitability, customer trends, product performance, and regional distribution. The dashboard transforms corporate sales data into actionable business insights, enabling decision-makers to monitor key performance indicators, evaluate operational efficiency, and identify opportunities for growth.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Preview

![Sales Dashboard Preview](./dashboard_preview.png)

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales, cost, and profit performance.
- Monitor revenue trends across multiple years.
- Evaluate product category performance.
- Identify top-performing customers.
- Compare sales distribution across regions.
- Analyze shipping modes and order trends over time.
- Support business decision-making through interactive visualizations.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Executive Summary
Provides a high-level overview of business performance through KPI cards.

**Key Metrics**
- **Total Sales:** `$1.3M`
- **Total Cost:** `$640.6K`
- **Total Profit:** `$627.0K`
- **Interactive Date Range:** `01-Jan-2017` to `31-Dec-2020`

---

### 2. Customer Performance Analysis

**Dashboard Highlights**
- Customer-wise Sales, Cost, and Profit analysis.
- Interactive matrix displaying financial performance for individual customers.
- Helps identify high-value and profitable customers.

---

### 3. Product Performance

**Dashboard Highlights**
- Sales by Product Sub-Category.
- Comparison of product demand across different categories.
- Top-performing product categories include:
  - Bookcases
  - Chairs
  - Appliances

---

### 4. Regional Sales Analysis

**Dashboard Highlights**
- Quantity distribution across business regions.
- Interactive pie chart comparing regional contributions.
- Geographic map visualizing:
  - Sales
  - Quantity
  - Cost
  - Profit

**Regional Distribution**
- Central — **55.11%**
- South — **22.72%**
- North — **22.17%**

---

### 5. Shipping & Order Trend Analysis

**Dashboard Highlights**
- Time-series analysis of order activity.
- Shipping mode comparison across multiple years.
- Tracks seasonal order trends by:
  - Economy
  - Economy Plus
  - Immediate
  - Priority

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Generated **$1.3M** in total sales with **$627K** in net profit.
- The **Central** region contributed more than half of the total quantity sold.
- Bookcases, Chairs, and Appliances were the leading product categories.
- Customer-level financial analysis helps identify profitable client segments.
- Shipping trends reveal seasonal fluctuations and preferred delivery methods.
- Interactive filtering enables detailed exploration of sales performance across different dimensions.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Visualization Tool:** Power BI Desktop
- **Data Transformation:** Power Query
- **Data Analysis:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- **Visualizations:** KPI Cards, Bar Charts, Pie Charts, Maps, Line Charts, Matrix Tables, Slicers
- **Dashboard Design:** Interactive executive dashboard with cross-filtering and drill-down capabilities
