# SN Corp Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

An interactive, single-page Power BI dashboard designed to monitor and evaluate corporate sales performance, profitability channels, and regional fulfillment dynamics. This operational summary tracks key financial health indicators and helps decision-makers identify top customer cohorts, product performance, and shipping configurations.

## 🚀 Key Features & Insights

The single-page dashboard provides an executive lens into multi-year sales transactions:

### 📊 Executive Summary KPIs
*   **Total Revenue (Sales):** `$1.3M` gross volume generated.
*   **Total Operational Costs:** `$640.6K`.
*   **Net Profit:** `$627.0K` in total realized earnings.
*   **Analysis Timeline:** Interactive date range slicer filtering data from **01-01-2017 to 31-12-2020**.

### 📈 Operational & Regional Breakdowns
*   **Financials by Customer:** Comprehensive ledger matrix calculating distinct Sales, Profits, and Costs against individual client profiles (e.g., Adam Bentley, Aaron Bootman).
*   **Product Performance (Sales by Sub-Category):** Vertical bar chart tracking inventory demand, revealing **Bookcases**, **Chairs**, and **Appliances** as major top-line volume drivers.
*   **Geographic Distribution (Quantity by Region):** Pie chart showing core item distribution, heavily led by the **Central** region (`55.11%`), followed by **South** (`22.72%`) and **North** (`22.17%`).
*   **Geographic Map Plotting:** Spatial map tracking sales, quantity, cost, and profit counts dynamically localized across primary European territories.
*   **Logistics Timeline Trend:** Time-series line chart tracking Order IDs across years and quarters, split by fulfillment channel choices (**Economy**, **Economy Plus**, **Immediate**, and **Priority**), showing seasonal growth spikes across standard shipping classes.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools

*   **BI Tool:** Power BI Desktop
*   **Core Concepts:** Financial metric calculations (Sales/Cost/Profit margins), cross-filtering, and seasonal timeline tracking.
*   **Visual Design:** Bright color-blocked KPI cards (Blue/Orange/Green) for rapid executive evaluation against a high-contrast workspace.
