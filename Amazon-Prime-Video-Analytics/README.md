# Amazon Prime Video Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to explore and analyze the comprehensive catalog of Amazon Prime Video titles. This dashboard transforms raw content data into clear insights regarding audience ratings, genre distributions, and production trends over time.

---

## 🚀 Key Features & Insights

The single-page dashboard focuses on content tracking and distribution matrices:

### 📊 Executive Summary KPIs
*   **Total Titles:** `9.655K` distinct shows and movies cataloged.
*   **Total Ratings:** `25` unique content classification categories.
*   **Total Genres:** `519` localized genre combinations.
*   **Total Directors:** `5.771K` creative directors tracked.

### 📈 Analytical Breakdowns
*   **Content Type Split (Movies vs. TV Shows):** An interactive donut chart highlighting that the catalog is heavily dominated by Movies (`7.81K` / 80.82%) compared to TV Shows (`1.85K` / 19.18%).
*   **Ratings by Total Shows:** Horizontal bar chart showcasing maturity rating groups, indicating high volume concentrations around `13+`, `16+`, `ALL`, and `18+` content classifications.
*   **Genres By Total Shows:** Ranking analysis highlighting core popular genres like `Drama`, `Comedy`, and suspense subcategories.
*   **Historical Timeline Analysis:** A dual area/line chart tracking the growth of catalog listings by `release_year` and content `type`, visualizing a massive production spike leading toward the 2000s and beyond.
